Program Objectives (Computer Engineering)

The program teaches graduates to apply mathematics and science to solve complex engineering problems, design systems, and analyze data. It prepares graduates to work in multidisciplinary teams, address engineering challenges professionally and ethically, and utilize modern tools and techniques. Graduates can specialize in areas like electronics engineering.

Program Objectives (Electronics Engineering)

Electronic Engineers use mathematics and science to solve problems, design, and analyze data, while considering economic, environmental, social, and ethical constraints. They work in multidisciplinary teams, understand global impact, and integrate engineering and management principles.
