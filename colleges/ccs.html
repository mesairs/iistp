Program Objectives

  1. Prepare students to be leaders in their profession, in their communities, and society.
  2. Prepare students to be successful IT professionals, and to be proficient in designing.
  3. Produce System Developers, Software Engineers, Systems Analysts, Database Designers, Web Developers and Computer Science Instructors.
  4. Produce graduates who are responsible members of society with an awareness of the social and ethical ramifications of their work.
  5. Produce graduates who have good communication skills and strong analytical skills so that they can solve problems and communicate their solution.
