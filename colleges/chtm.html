⁍ Bachelor of Science of Hotel and Restaurant Management

Program Objectives (Hotel and Restaurant Management)

Apply knowledge and skills learned in the classroom into novel situations in the workplace in the following areas:

  • Room reservations
  • Marketing and Sales and Customer Relations
  • Food Service Sanitation, Purchasing and Storage
  • Menu Planning, Food Preparation, and Restaurant and Banquet Services
  • Display professional commitment to serve in the hospitality industry
  • Hospitality Accounting, Cost Controls, and Legal Aspects and Insurance

Program Objectives (Tourism Management)

  1. Promote the Philippines' best through tourism.
  2. Specialize on the growing adventure, historical, eco-tourism.
  3. Join the world's most exciting, biggest and fastest growing industry in the country and across the globe.
  4. Participate in a highly changing world and even change the way we look at our country and our world.
  5. Experience not merely a job, but a variety of cultural and entertaining activities, sights, music, arts and good food.
  6. Acquire the skill and knowledge in tourism destination and tour operations and management and a variety of tourism and tourism related businees

Possible Careers

Hospitality Management:

  • Academician
  • Front office staff
  • Management trainee
  • Executive housekeeper
  • Food and beverage director
  • Restaurant operations manager
  • Leisure and recreation consultant
  • Restaurant or lodging business owner

Tourism Management:

  • Hotel Manager
  • Events Manager
  • Tour Group Operator
  • Travel Agency Manager
  • Guest Relations Manager
  • Tourism Marketing Manager
  • Tourist Information Centre Manager
