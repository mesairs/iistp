Program Objectives

  1. Develop intellectual, personal, interpersonal, communication, organizational and management skills of the Accountancy students required in the local and international industry.
  2. Provide opportunities for their growth and development as well as the practice of their professions in any of the four sectors: education, public practice, government and commerce and industry in conformity with the standards of work ethics and professionalism.
  3. Make and keep on making discoveries and researches in the field of Accounting for its improvement and as an answer to present needs of the society.
  4. Provide foundation of professional knowledge, skills, values, ethics, and attitude to pass the CPA Licensure Examination.
  5. Active involvement in community service towards the improvement and development of the society.
