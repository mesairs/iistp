Program Objectives (E.E/S.E)

  1. To develop communication skills and use the modern information knowledge.
  2. To enable the prospective teacher to understand the nature, purpose, philosophy of elementary and secondary educations.
  3. To enable them to have creative thinking among students for reconstruction of knowledge.
  4. To provide quality education by being an agent of change in the individuals total personality.
  5. To equip educators with competencies relevant to stage specific pedagogy, curriculum development transaction and evaluation.
  6. To support the needs of the industry, thus creating new program to help the student become more productive.

Program Objectives (Communication)

This program aims to equip students with the knowledge and skills in the theory and practice of communication applicable in various professions and contexts, with focus on the media professions.
