Program Objectives

  1. Develop among the students the competencies and skills as entrepreneurs, managers and executives.
  2. Equip the students with knowledge of the principles, concepts, theories, philosophies of financial management, marketing management, operations management, human resource development management, banking and management accounting.
  3. Make and keep on making discoveries and researches in the field of Accounting for its improvement and as an answer to present needs of the society.
  4. Prepare the students to seek employment and assume entry-level jobs or positions in their respective fields of specialization, either in the local or global business community.
  5. Prepare the students to pursue a teaching career or graduate studies in business.

Possible Careers

  • Accountant
  • City Manager
  • Chief Executive Officer
  • Commercial Loan Officer
  • Financial Officer
  • Sales Manager
  • Advertising Executive
  • Human Resources Manager
  • Public Relation Specialist
