NKP | Nursery, Kindergarten, Preparatory

N |Ages 2-3

The primary focus is on fostering a nurturing environment where children can explore and learn through play-based activities. Emphasis is placed on developing social skills, language acquisition, fine and gross motor skills, and basic cognitive abilities.

K |Ages 4-5

The curriculum builds upon the foundational skills introduced in Nursery and prepares children for formal schooling. Activities are structured to promote independent learning, problem-solving, creativity, and collaboration.

P |Ages 5-6

The curriculum is designed to further develop academic readiness skills, including reading, writing, mathematics, and science, while continuing to foster social-emotional growth and independence.

Business High School

The Regular/Business High School complies with the Basic Education Curriculum (BEC) prescribed by Department of Education. SHS | SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL

ABM

ABM is the short term for Accountancy, Business and Management. The Accountancy, Business and Management (ABM) strand would focus on the basic concepts of financial management, business management, corporate operations, and all things that are accounted for

STEM

The STEM strand in Senior High School is designed for students who aspire to pursue a career in fields related to Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. This strand offers a robust curriculum that develops analytical skills, critical thinking, and practical problem-solving abilities.

TVL

TVL is the short term for Technical-Vocational-Livelihood Track and one of the offered to Senior High School Students. This strand is for learners who aim to take up in the field of Electronic Sector, Tourism Sector and Automotive Sector.

HUMSS

Humanities and Social Sciences Track. This strand is for learners who aim to take up journalism, communication arts, liberal arts, education and other social science related courses in college.
