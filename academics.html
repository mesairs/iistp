Requirements

Requirements for Kinder:

  • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate

Requirements for Kinder 2 (Transferee)

(5 years old - October 31):

  • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
  • Certificate of Completion (Kinder 1)

Requirements for Grade 1 (Transferee):

  • ECD Form
  • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
  • Certificate of Completion (Kinder 2)

Requirements for Grade 2 to Grade 6 (Transferee):

  • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
  • Report Card (Original)

Requirements for Junior High School:

  • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
  • Grade 6 Report Card (Original)

Requirements for Educational Service Contracting (ESC):

  • ESC Application Form provided by their Advisers
  • Recent Identical 2x2 I.D. Photo with white background (2 Copies)
  • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
  • Grade 6 Report Card (Original)
  • Barangay Certificate
    • Student's Full Name
    • Parent's Name and Current Employment
    • Monthly Income

Requirements for Senior High School:

  • Report Card (Original)
  • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • 2x2 I.D. Photo with white background (2 Copies)

      For SHS Voucher Recipient(From Private School)

    • ESC/QVA Certificate

Requirements for College (Freshmen):

  • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Senior High School Report Card (Form 138)
  • Form 137 (Required after officially enrolled)
  • 2x2 I.D. Photo with white background (2 Copies)

Requirements for College (Transferee):

  • Honorable Dismissal
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • 2x2 I.D. Photo with white background (2 Copies)
  • Transcript of Records or Certification of Grades for Evaluation

    • Required after officially enrolled:

  • Transcript of Records with Remarks (Original)
      *Copy for Imus Institute of Science and Technology, Inc.

Requirements for Educational Units / Master in Business Administration:

  • Barangay Clearance
  • Honorable Dismissal
  • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
  • Marriage Certificate (PSA) (If married female)
  • 2x2 I.D. Photo with white background (2 Copies)
  • Transcript of Records or Certification of Grades for Evaluation (Original)

    • Required after officially enrolled:

  • Transcript of Records with Remarks (Original)
      *Copy for Imus Institute of Science and Technology, Inc.
